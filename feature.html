Featured Friends of the Week

At the Animal Humane, we believe that every pet is a featured friend!
This week, our spotlight shines brightly on these pet-friends.
If you are thinking about a pet, please consider one of these worthy animals.

Donate

Neptune is a super sweet black and tan Lancaster Heeler mix who loves to snuggle and play with toys. Neptune would love to go home with you but he doesn’t really want to share you with any other animals. Neptune has had a lot of basic training already and is housebroken.


Adopt

Laurel and Hardy are the most humorous and adorable pair of bunnies that you’ll ever meet! Perhaps that’s a big statement, but they’re certain they can live up to their stage names. They are very friendly bunnies. Laurel prefers cuddles over Hardy, but they are both happy to be held.


Adopt

Cookie is a 2-3 year old “chug”, a chihuahua & pug mix. She is a tiny package of personality! Cookie is housebroken and crate trained. She loves other dogs, and would be happiest with a play partner or two! Cookie had a hip injury, but she has had surgery to help eliminate the pain. It didn’t slow her down before the surgery, nor has it since! She loves to run and jump. She is sweet and cuddly, and will make a wonderful family member!


Adopt

Millie is a loving companion, but she is somewhat shy. A quieter, low-activity home would allow her the opportunity to get used to new things at her own pace. Millie is very independent and enjoys exploring her surroundings. She also likes toys and activities to keep herself busy. Millie does not demand cuddles and kisses, but likes being part of your daily activities. Families with older children are especially suitable for Millie.
