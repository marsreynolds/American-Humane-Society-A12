Featured Friends of the Week

At the Animal Humane, we believe that every pet is a featured friend!
This week, our spotlight shines brightly on these pet-friends.
If you are thinking about a pet, please consider one of these worthy animals.

Adopt

Neptune is a super sweet black and tan Lancaster Heeler mix who loves to snuggle and play with toys. Neptune would love to go home with you but he doesn’t really want to share you with any other animals. Neptune has had a lot of basic training already and is housebroken.


Sponsor

Laurel and Hardy are the most humorous and adorable pair of bunnies that you’ll ever meet! Perhaps that’s a big statement, but they’re certain they can live up to their stage names. They are very friendly bunnies. Laurel prefers cuddles over Hardy, but they are both happy to be held.


Sponsor

Cookie is a 2-3 year old “chug”, a chihuahua & pug mix. She is a tiny package of personality! Cookie is housebroken and crate trained. She loves other dogs, and would be happiest with a play partner! She loves to run and jump. She is sweet and cuddly, and will make a wonderful family member!


Sponsor

Millie is a loving companion, but she is shy. A quieter, low-activity home would allow her to get used to new things at her own pace. Millie is independent, likes toys. and enjoys exploring her surroundings. Families with older children are especially suitable for Millie.
